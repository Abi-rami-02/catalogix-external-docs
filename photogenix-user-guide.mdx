## Using Photogenix

<Info>
Here‚Äôs how to generate, customize, and download your visuals inside Photogenix.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Images">
  Follow on-screen instructions to upload your product or model images for your chosen use case.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Edits">
  Choose your desired model, background, and styling options from the Photogenix library or create your own prompts.
  </Step>

  Click **Generate** to process the image using AI. Photogenix will render your visuals within seconds.

  Go to the **Renders** tab to view and download your processed images. Export in marketplace-specific dimensions.

## Additional Features

<Expandable title="Regenerate">
To change the model or background in generated images:
1. Click **Regenerate** on your image.
2. Update your selections from the model or background library.
3. Click **Generate** again to create a new version.

<Note>
Each regeneration counts as a new image and will deduct credits from your plan.
</Note>

![Regenerate Option](https://assets.catalogix.ai/f11eab894bc8b4d5b13f1d8c96bdefe4)
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Retouch">
