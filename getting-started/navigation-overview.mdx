The CXO Retail Command Center is designed to give you quick access to everything you need — apps, teams, and billing.

- **Left Panel**: Access all Streamoid apps — *ArtifaX*, *PhotogeniX*, *CatalogiX*.
- **Teams**: Manage members and permissions.
- **Billing**: View and manage credits and invoices.
