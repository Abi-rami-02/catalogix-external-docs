<Info>
Collaborate with your team securely by inviting members, assigning roles, and managing app access.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invite a Team Member">
  Go to the **Teams** tab and click **Invite User**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Permissions">
  Choose the memberâ€™s role (Admin or Member) and toggle credit or invite permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign App Access">
  Select which apps (ArtifaX, PhotogeniX, CatalogiX) the user can access, then click **Invite**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Always assign the least required role. Group members by responsibility and review roles regularly for security.
</Note>
