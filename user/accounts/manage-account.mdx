Title: "Creating Account" <br />
Description: "Creating an account as a new user on Catalogix"

**Steps for creating an account on Catalogix**

1. Go to the url: [https://dashboard.catalogix.ai/](https://dashboard.catalogix.ai/)
2. Enter your e-mail id for registering on the tool.
3. Enter the OTP received on your registered e-mail id.
4. Enter your name and your company's name and click on create.
5. Congratulations your account is now ready to use.
