## Creating and Managing Stores

<Info>
A **Store** in Catalogix is a dedicated workspace where you upload, organize, and manage your product catalogs independently.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Store">
  Click the **+ button** under *Create New Store* to start setting up your store.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Store Details">
  Add a store name, region, and select your selling type â€” *Marketplace*, *Direct to Customers (D2C)*, or both.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Channels">
  For Marketplace stores, select the platforms you wish to publish on (e.g., Amazon, Myntra, Ajio, etc.). For D2C, enter your website link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use Advanced Settings">
  Connect an existing store or choose a default language.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create & Proceed">
  After confirming all details, click **Create & Proceed** to set up your store.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Uploading Products

<Info>
You can add products to your store through uploads, integrations, or manual entry.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Products">
  Click **Add Products** and choose one of the following:
  - Upload a Catalog File (.csv, .xls, .xlsx, .zip)  
  - Connect Website or Feed  
  - Create in Catalogix manually
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Channel and Mapping">
  Choose the appropriate channel (e.g., SMP, Amazon, etc.) and select **Auto Mapping** to match your data columns automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map Attributes and Values">
  Verify attributes (like SKU, Product Name, Description) and check mapped values for accuracy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Repair and Finalize">
  Fix any missing or incorrect data and click **Proceed** to finish uploading.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Store Settings

<Info>
The **Store Settings** panel lets you configure store details, automations, imports, exports, and taxonomy curation.
</Info>

<Expandable title="General Settings">
- View or edit **Store Details**: Name, Region, and Business Type.  
- Access **Advanced Settings** for deeper configurations.  
- Check **Channel Versions** for sync status.  
- Update, delete, or save changes.  
![General Settings](https://assets.catalogix.ai/444fe73ee7c67c08ab2cf34e28ddaf19)
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Automation">
Automate tasks like:  
- Analyze Image/Text  
- Auto QC  
- Extract Attributes from Text  
- Generate Attributes from Images  
- Identify Image Type  
- Remove Background  
![Automation Settings](https://assets.catalogix.ai/3083071637f12c58cbd5b7696f6bff0c)
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Import">
Manage product imports and feeds.  
- View active feeds and import history.  
- Use **Add/Import Feed** to define source, frequency, and status.  
![Import Settings](https://assets.catalogix.ai/c648d2cc04e75c52e11dcc16cf69cd3d)
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Export">
Control data syncing and outbound automation.  
- Add new sync rules using **Add New Sync**.  
- Define rule name, frequency, and channel.  
![Export Settings](https://assets.catalogix.ai/de611fc1ac3fa9efd5203f5288346d9d)
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Curation">
Refine your taxonomy for better AI accuracy.  
- Choose from 400+ attributes and 250+ taxonomy values.  
- Apply filters by category (e.g., Apparel, Beauty, Home).  
![Curation Settings](https://assets.catalogix.ai/849ffcb3ca03acea00ea98472eed7275)
</Expandable>
