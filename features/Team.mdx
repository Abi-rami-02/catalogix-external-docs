## **Overview**

Team is a feature in Catalogix that allows multiple users to work together on same store/workspace. The tool allows users to add members to the teams, grant/revoke  access to specific parts of a store.

**Steps for inviting new members to your team.**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Teams Section">
    You can access the invite members to your team by clicking on team button on the left.
<Frame caption="Teams section">
  <img src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/9891648019b8a0c2a6742282a221ae69" />
</Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite New User">
    Then for adding a new user you can click on Invite new user button the right
<Frame caption="Invite new user">
  <img src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/45ab082d4159e152fa324d5bec4a5d8a" />
</Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="User e-mail and access">
    After that you need to enter the e-mail of the member you want to add to the the team. You should specify the access that needs to be given to the member and then click on Invite .
<Frame caption="User e-mail and access">
  <img src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/c9183d9643cd30b1f7b95b5c89acecc2" />
</Frame>
  </Step>
    <Step title="E-Mail Invite">
    The invited member will get an invitation to join the team on the specified e-mail. The user needs to click on the view invitation button.
<Frame caption="E-mail invitation">
  <img src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/a3621195ee99e4a46cf1639410e21d65" />
</Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Accept Invite">
    Then the user will be redirected to catalogix where they can click on Yes,accept to get added to the team.
<Frame caption="Accept invite on catalogix">
  <img src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/7555b71bb6e0547d4b48e900c0ceadb1" />
</Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Member Added">
    The member is now added to the team.
<Frame caption="Member added to the team">
  <img src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/9368b6b5d5393a95c3d8153705b25ac0" />
</Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
