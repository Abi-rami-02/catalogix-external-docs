**Steps for creating a new store on Catalogix.**

1. For creating a new store, go to the **store** section under the workspace and then click on the **Create New Store** button.
<Frame caption="Create new store">
  <img src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/cd2485157bf31c39c35c1055c191836e"/>
</Frame>
<br />
2. Enter a name for the store.
<Frame caption="Name the store">
  <img src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/95bb354a8e5c42db34baa0dd8430bff7" />
</Frame>
<br />
3. Select a region in which you want to sell.
<Frame caption="Select the region">
  <img src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/65e0de6bc8c86619821100b347981fec" />
</Frame>
<br />
4. Select the mode the in which you sell to your customers. You have to select at least one mode but you can select multiple modes as well.
<Frame caption="Select how you sell to your customers">
  <img src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/1bde61c160e11629b4d0a19d81f3c2e4" />
</Frame>
<br />
5. Select the channels where you sell your products. You can select multiple options.
<Frame caption="Select how you sell to your customers">
  <img src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/9929c86909305040baf050e2aefa4fbb" />
</Frame>
<br />
6. Select the language that you use for your e-commerce listing.
<Frame caption="Select your language">
  <img src= "https://assets.catalogix.ai/52612c3ad88768656767c7328dc10c31" />
</Frame>
<br />
7. Click on create and proceed. Your store is ready to use.
<Frame caption="Store is ready to use">
  <img src= "https://assets.catalogix.ai/866acba57d9ce7de3f0aae1b43359814" />
</Frame>
