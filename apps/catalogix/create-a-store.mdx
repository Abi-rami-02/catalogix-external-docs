---
title: "Create a Store"
description: "Set up a new store with the right regions, channels, and advanced options."
---

<Tip>
Use separate stores for different brands, product categories, or sales regions to keep catalogs organized.
</Tip>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Store">
  Click the **+ button** under *Create New Store* to start setting up your store.![Create Store](https://assets.catalogix.ai/89b2fb2c87aeec59072a8aa91fd7c625)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Store Details">
  Add a store name, region, and select your selling type as *Marketplace* ![Store Details](https://assets.catalogix.ai/5cecbff5607003c09a5613e3492b491c)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Channels">
  For Marketplace stores, select the platforms you wish to publish on (e.g., Amazon, Myntra, Ajio, etc.).![Channels](https://assets.catalogix.ai/b69f795d57d375bd82655605475b567b)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create & Proceed">
  After confirming all details, click **Create & Proceed** to set up your store. ![Created Store](https://assets.catalogix.ai/454ebf7c5afca68883086add60a2365e)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Always select *marketplace* as your selling type. The D2C option requires set-up. 
</Tip>
