## Move from one app to another

<Info>
You can move seamlessly between Artifax, Photogenix, and Catalogix within CXO. 
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Apps Menu">
  Click the **four-cube icon** next to the app label.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose Your App">
  Select from Artifax, PhotogeniX, or CatalogiX â€” click the arrow icon to switch instantly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Return to Dashboard">

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
All apps share your workspace and billing, so transitions are instant and smooth.
