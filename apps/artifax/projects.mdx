title: "Projects"
description: "Organize, remix, and iterate on creative assets inside Artifax Projects."
---

<Info>
Projects are your creative library — collect, remix, and iterate all in one place.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Projects">
  Click **Projects** from the left-hand navigation panel.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Image">
  Add new designs, sketches, or inspirations directly into your workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Remix or Annotate">
  Open any saved image to remix, add notes, or generate design variations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Use Projects as your personal moodboard and design archive — everything stays synced and remix-ready.
</Tip>
