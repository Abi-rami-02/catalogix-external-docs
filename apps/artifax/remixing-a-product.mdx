---
title: "Remix a Product"
description: "Use Artifax Remix to generate fresh concepts from existing inspiration."
---

<Info>
Remix is Artifax’s signature creative tool — transform inspiration into unique, production-ready concepts.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Remix">
  From any product view, click the **Remix button** to open the creative editor.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Describe Your Idea">
  Add a short prompt describing changes or new ideas (e.g., “Add thunderbolt embroidery on the sleeve”).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Reference Images">
  Upload up to **four images** to guide the remix output visually.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Variations">
  Click **Generate (10 credits)** — Artifax creates new design variations instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
