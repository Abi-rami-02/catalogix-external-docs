<Info>
Scout is where inspiration begins — browse, search, filter, and remix designs directly from tracked brands.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Products">
  Use filters like **Popular**, **Newest**, or **Relevance** to refine results.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Apply Filters or Search">
  Combine filters (category, color, season, etc.) for precise exploration. You can also search for products using text or a reference image. 
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click a Product">
  Open detailed product views with attributes, insights, and remix options.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>

</Note>
