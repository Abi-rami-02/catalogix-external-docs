---
title: "Add Images to Projects"
description: "Save inspiration from Scout into Projects or upload your own."
---

To add products from Scout to your project, simply hover on a product and click on the save button. You can further choose which project you want to add the product to.

<img
  src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/300919efbdaacc2cf6510fb4e05d2054"
  alt="Save products to a project from the Scout grid"
  style={{ width: '100%', borderRadius: '0.75rem', margin: '1.5rem 0' }}
/>

If you have not created a project, you can create one on the go! After clicking on the save button, simply click on 'create new project' to start a new project!

<img
  src="https://assets.catalogix.ai/19c4cc143435dce206d5ca7309d5fc43"
  alt="Create a project from the save modal"
  style={{ width: '100%', borderRadius: '0.75rem', margin: '1.5rem 0' }}
/>

---

<Tip>
Upon choosing your brands, Scout automatically creates stories or groups of products that have similar visual features. These are called 'stories' and can be found at the top of the listing page. 
</Tip>
