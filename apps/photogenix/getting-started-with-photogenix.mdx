<Info>
You can access Photogenix directly from the Streamoid CXO dashboard or by visiting the Photogenix website — your Streamoid credentials unlock both.
</Info>

---

## Launch Photogenix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Your Account">
  Visit [https://photogenix.ai](https://photogenix.ai) and sign up with your email ID. Enter the verification code sent to your inbox.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Login from CXO">
  If you're using CXO, click the **Photogenix shortcut arrow** on the dashboard to launch instantly.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Designing">
  Once logged in, your account is ready — choose a use case and begin creating stunning visuals.
  </Step>
</Steps>
